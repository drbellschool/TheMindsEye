# Pilot School / Museum Partner Packet

This document is a planning packet for a limited Texarkana 1885 pilot.
It is not a contract, license, or public partnership announcement.

## Purpose

The Mind's Eye may eventually run a small pilot with a school partner and a
museum or historical organization partner. This packet defines the discussion
surface so the project can prepare without overcommitting.

## Pilot Shape

The intended pilot shape is narrow:

- one teacher;
- one class;
- one town package;
- one bounded mission flow;
- limited feedback;
- and no broad rollout assumptions.

## School Partner Discussion Points

The school-side conversation should focus on:

- grade band and course fit;
- teacher review expectations;
- classroom scheduling;
- accessibility needs;
- privacy expectations;
- and how feedback will be collected.

## Museum / Historical Partner Discussion Points

The museum or historical partner conversation should focus on:

- historical review of source interpretation;
- local context for people, places, and labels;
- source-rights awareness;
- and whether the partner can help validate historical framing.

## What The Project Offers

The project can provide:

- a teacher-reviewed town package;
- source-linked mission materials;
- provenance labels;
- map-linked evidence;
- and a repeatable, narrow pilot workflow.

## What The Project Does Not Offer

The pilot packet should make it clear that the project does not yet offer:

- production student accounts;
- district deployment;
- open-ended town onboarding;
- or a public commitment to any partner terms.

## Questions To Resolve Before A Pilot

- Who is the teacher or pilot contact?
- Who is the school administrative contact?
- Who is the museum or historical contact?
- What feedback will the pilot collect?
- What data, if any, will be retained?
- How will the partner name, logo, or screenshots be used?
- What human review happens before anything is public?

## Boundary Notes

- This packet does not grant rights.
- This packet does not change ownership.
- This packet does not choose a license.
- This packet does not authorize public distribution of partner materials.

## Current Status

The pilot partner packet is a discussion scaffold only. Any real pilot terms,
data sharing, or public-use permissions must be reviewed by the project owner
before use.
